ADDITIONAL RULES FOR BUDGET TRAVEL (style: budget):

ACCOMMODATION (35% of budget):
- Choose hostels, guesthouses, or budget hotels (₹500-1500/night)
- Dorms are great for solo travelers (₹400-800/night)
- Always mention free amenities (WiFi, breakfast included?)
- Suggest OYO Rooms, Zostel, or local guesthouses

FOOD (25% of budget):
- Prioritize street food and local dhabas
- Avoid hotel restaurants and tourist traps
- Mention specific cheap eats: thali meals ₹80-150, street snacks ₹20-50
- Self-catering tips: local markets for fruits/snacks

TRANSPORT (20% of budget):
- Always prefer local buses, metro, or shared auto over private cabs
- Mention bus/metro card tips (day passes often save money)
- Walk where distances are under 1.5km
- Use Ola Share / Uber Pool instead of regular

ACTIVITIES (15% of budget):
- Prioritize free attractions: parks, temples, viewpoints, beaches
- Mention free days / discounts (student ID, photography fees)
- Suggest free walking routes through old neighborhoods

TONE:
Frame this as an adventure — budget travel reveals the authentic side of the destination that luxury travelers miss.
