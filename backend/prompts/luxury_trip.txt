ADDITIONAL RULES FOR LUXURY TRAVEL (style: luxury):

ACCOMMODATION (35% of budget):
- Choose 5-star hotels, heritage properties, or boutique luxury resorts
- Mention specific luxury brands if appropriate (Oberoi, Taj, ITC, Marriott)
- Highlight exclusive amenities: pool, spa, rooftop dining, butler service
- Always justify why this property is the best choice for the location

FOOD (25% of budget):
- Named fine-dining restaurants only — no street food
- Curated dining experiences: chef's table, sunset dinners, heritage restaurant
- Mention signature dishes and wine pairing if relevant
- Include a mix of local cuisine done upscale and international fine dining

TRANSPORT (20% of budget):
- Private car with driver for all intercity and intracity travel
- Consider helicopter or private charter for scenic transfers where available
- First class or premium economy on trains if used
- No shared transport — all transfers should be exclusive

ACTIVITIES (15% of budget):
- Private guided tours over group tours
- After-hours museum access, exclusive experiences
- Spa days, cooking classes with renowned chefs, private boat tours
- Pre-booking all tickets to skip queues entirely

TONE:
Frame this as a curated, once-in-a-lifetime experience. Emphasize exclusivity, personalization, and the feeling of being truly taken care of.
